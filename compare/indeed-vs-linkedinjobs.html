Job Boards

Indeed

General-purpose job search engine aggregating listings from many sources

Pricing: Free

Pros

  • Massive listing volume across every industry
  • Simple one-click apply on many posts

Cons

  • Listing quality is inconsistent, some are stale or reposted

LinkedIn Jobs

The largest professional network's native job board

Pricing: Free / Premium subscription for extra insights

Pros

  • Largest pool of listings tied to real companies
  • "Easy Apply" reduces application friction

Cons

  • High competition per listing due to Easy Apply volume
