Indeed vs ZipRecruiter

Job Boards

Indeed

General-purpose job search engine aggregating listings from many sources

Pricing: Free

Pros

  • Massive listing volume across every industry
  • Simple one-click apply on many posts

Cons

  • Listing quality is inconsistent, some are stale or reposted

ZipRecruiter

Job board that actively pushes your profile to matching employers

Pricing: Free for job seekers

Pros

  • Employers often reach out first
  • Mobile app is well built for quick applications

Cons

  • Can generate a lot of email/notification noise
