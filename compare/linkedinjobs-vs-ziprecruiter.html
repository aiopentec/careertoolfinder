LinkedIn Jobs vs ZipRecruiter

Job Boards

LinkedIn Jobs

The largest professional network's native job board

Pricing: Free / Premium subscription for extra insights

Pros

  • Largest pool of listings tied to real companies
  • "Easy Apply" reduces application friction

Cons

  • High competition per listing due to Easy Apply volume

ZipRecruiter

Job board that actively pushes your profile to matching employers

Pricing: Free for job seekers

Pros

  • Employers often reach out first
  • Mobile app is well built for quick applications

Cons

  • Can generate a lot of email/notification noise
